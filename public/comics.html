New plant based comic

Alot of comics are things I've thought about for years and years. Things I daydream about, and this is no exception. The comic is set in Modern day northen Ireland like we are (because I'm creative) and it focuses on the 5 main characters; Evan, Flo, Dee, Tam and Luci. I don't want to pick favourites , but like my parents I will and its Flo. I've been thinking about every aspect of Flo for years and they were in one of my stories I wrote as a kid and I liked them so much that I added them into this comic. Flo is a sentient plant person who has been running the same top florist for a few hundred years. They are simple minded and pleasant and are already my favourite , so if you know which character gets more development , I think we both know what's up.

Anyways more on the actual comic, the writer will be me, but you don't know who I am so that's kinda pointless and the artist is also someone you don't know, so I hope you're all clued in. The plot is a battle againist the forces of evil namely Luci's siblings. The team will have to defeat every single one to stop the family business taking over the earth. I don't know how many siblings to put in but I hope it's not 7 because I would definitely ripping off Scott Pligrim (If you don't know what that is, it's the best comic ever and I would proabably monetize this site just to buy the card game) Anyways more updates and comics coming soon and you might see an issue of whatever this comic will be called soon.

So yeah look out for that please. Thanks.
